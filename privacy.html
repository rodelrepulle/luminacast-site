Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

LuminaCast is built privacy-first. Your photos and videos are yours, and the core editing experience runs entirely on your device. This policy explains what we do — and mostly don't — collect.

Your media never leaves your phone

All editing, color grading (LUT application), adjustments, and export happen 100% on-device. Photos and videos you import are copied into LuminaCast's private, encrypted app storage so editing works offline and survives the original being deleted. We never upload, transmit, or have access to your media.

Account & sign-in (optional)

Signing in is optional and only exists to let a Pro subscription follow you across your devices. If you choose to sign in (Apple, Google, Facebook, or email), we store the minimum needed for authentication — typically an email address and an account identifier — through our authentication provider (Supabase). You can sign out at any time, and you can use the entire app without ever creating an account.

Purchases

Subscriptions and the lifetime unlock are processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. Apple shares with us only the receipt/entitlement status needed to unlock Pro features.

Analytics (off by default)

LuminaCast collects no analytics unless you explicitly opt in under Settings → "Share anonymous usage." If enabled, we collect only coarse, anonymized counts (for example, how often a look is applied) to improve the app. These events never include your photos, media, names, or any personal data. "Ghost Mode" disables all analytics and optional cloud features entirely, regardless of other settings.

No third-party tracking or ads

We do not use advertising SDKs, we do not sell or share your data, and we do not track you across apps or websites.

Children

LuminaCast is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Your rights

Because your content lives on your device, you control it directly — deleting a story or the app removes its data. If you signed in and want your account data deleted, contact us and we will remove it.
