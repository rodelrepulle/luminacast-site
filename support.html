Frequently asked

Do my photos get uploaded?

No. All editing and export happen entirely on your device. Your media never leaves your phone.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Open the app, go to Profile → Subscription → "Restore Purchases," or Settings → Subscription. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Go to your iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → LuminaCast, or use Profile → Subscription → "Manage Subscription" in the app.

Can I use my own LUTs?

Yes. LuminaCast imports Adobe/Resolve .cube files via the Files app or AirDrop ("Open in LuminaCast"). Imported looks live in My Casts.
